5. I have no idea what all these terms mean, HELP ME!
    -To be honest you don’t have to worry about all the technical terms, however, let’s explain some of those:
      a. PC – personal computer, includes laptop and desktop computers.
      b. CPU- central processing unit, it is the brain of your computer.
      c. RAM- Random Access Memory, it is a type of storage.
      d. Hard Drive- this is where your pictures are stored.
      e. SSD- solid state drive, this is another type of hardrive. SUPER FAST!
      f. PSU- power supply unit, provides power to your desktop computer.
      g. Motherboard- this allows all the components in your computer to work in harmony.
      h. Virus/Spyware- nasty stuff that you don’t want in your computer, can steal your data, corrupt your files and much more.
